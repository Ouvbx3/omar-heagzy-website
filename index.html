Who's omar hegazy

Omar Hegazy is an Egyptian banker turned pro-athlete. After losing his leg in a 2015 motorcycle crash, he refused to stop moving. He became the first amputee to swim from Jordan to Egypt (20km) and broke two Guinness World Records for underwater swimming on a single breath. He now spends his time as a motivational speaker, proving that "limitation" is just a mindset.
